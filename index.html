Our story

Started around a simple hearth, and a simple idea

Kitoko Hearth began with a small group of young people who wanted more than theory — they wanted to build things that worked, and see them out in the world. That idea grew into a program, then a community, then a home for anyone ready to learn technology the way it's actually practiced.
