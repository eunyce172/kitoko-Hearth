You're in the circle now.

Thank you for applying to Kitoko Hearth. Our team reviews every application by hand — you'll hear back from us at the email you provided within one week.

What happens next

  1. We review your application. Every submission is read by a real person on our team.
  2. We reach out by email. If it's a good fit, we'll schedule a short, friendly welcome call.
  3. You join the cohort. Welcome to the hearth — let's start building.
